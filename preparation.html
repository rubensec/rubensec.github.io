Rubensec presents OSCP Preparation

Here you will find tutorials and information related to OSCP. OSCP is a cybersecurity certificate provided by Offensive Security (the company behind Kali Linux OS). In this section I will link posts updated in this same page, so hope this page to be constantly changing. Posts will cover a wide variety of content and will explain those things I don't find necessary to upload a video. I don't have OSCP so I will try to explain and cover all things I would find useful. The reason for me to do this is force myself to keep learning and prepare myself for the OSCP exam, so feel free to join me on the ride!
